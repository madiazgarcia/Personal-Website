Teaching.

Occasionally I try to inspire other people to learn something - but see for yourselves.

Seminar: "Chances and Limitations of Descriptive Political Representation"

Course Description: The seminar examines the importance of the descriptive representation of socially marginalized groups for political attitudes and behavior of citizens and elites, including the normative debate about its desirable role. Further, it examines the effects of descriptive representation on attitudes that support democracy and on substantive representation (German).
Goethe-University Frankfurt am Main
Winter term 2024/2025 & Summer Term 2025

Syllabus. Evaluation.

Seminar: "Democracy and Migration"

Course Description: The seminar explores the impact of international migration on social cohesion and political culture, particularly in the context of liberalism and demarcation. It examines classical concepts of migration research, questions of citizenship, integration, political participation, country-specific policies, and societal responses to migration (German).
Goethe-University Frankfurt am Main
Summer Term 2025
